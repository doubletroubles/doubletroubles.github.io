Double Troubles

NYC Swing Choreography Troupe

About

The Double Troubles is a NYC-based swing choreography troupe. Our vision is to create performances that explore ambidancetrous swing dancing, so that the world can see more examples of swing dancers who both lead and follow and who dance with same-sex partners.

There are no auditions for rehearsals! We are an open performance group, and anyone who wants to commit the time to come to rehearsals is welcome to come practice with us, hopefully learning a few things and making new friends in the process. The choreographer of each piece will then cast each performance from within the troupe.

We hope to dance with you soon!

Performances

The Double Troubles perform regularly at various venues in the New York City area for audiences of dancers, and non-dancers alike. We can perform our 3 minute long pieces at your next party, or event. We can also teach our piece at your event to dancers that have some knowledge of Lindy hop basics. If you'd like the double troubles to perform, please email us at doubletroubles@gmail.com.
